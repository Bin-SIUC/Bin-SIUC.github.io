About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at Southern Illinois University Carbondale since 2023. I received my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Virginia Tech in 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Robert G. Parker, and was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Utah from 2019–2022. My research integrates group theory, nonlinear dynamics, and machine learning to analyze and control complex mechanical systems, with applications in vibration analysis, energy harvesting, and additive manufacturing.
